# Personal Loan - Product Brochure

## Overview
DemoBank Personal Loan offers quick and hassle-free financing for your personal needs. Whether it's a wedding, medical emergency, home renovation, or travel, we've got you covered.

## Key Features
- Loan Amount: ₹50,000 to ₹10,00,000
- Tenure: 12 to 60 months
- Interest Rate: 10.5% - 18% p.a. (based on credit profile)
- Processing Fee: 2% of loan amount (min ₹1,000)
- No prepayment charges after 6 months

## Eligibility Criteria
- Minimum Age: 21 years
- Maximum Age: 60 years
- Minimum Monthly Income: ₹15,000 for salaried individuals
- Employment: Minimum 2 years work experience
- Credit Score: 650+ preferred
