# MSME Business Loan - Product Brochure

## Overview
DemoBank MSME Business Loan provides financial support to micro, small, and medium enterprises. Get working capital, machinery loans, or expansion funding for your business.

## Key Features
- Loan Amount: ₹1,00,000 to ₹2,00,00,000
- Tenure: Up to 10 years (term loan), overdraft facility available
- Interest Rate: 10% - 15% p.a.
- Processing Fee: 1.5% of loan amount
- Collateral required for loans above ₹10 lakhs
- Government subsidy schemes applicable
- Working capital and term loan both available

## Eligibility Criteria
- Business vintage: Minimum 3 years
- Minimum Annual Turnover: ₹10 lakhs
- Maximum Annual Turnover: ₹250 crores (as per MSME definition)
- Promoter age: 21-65 years
- GST registration mandatory
- ITR filing for last 2 years
- Credit Score: 650+

## Loan Types
- **Working Capital Loan**: For daily operations, inventory, raw materials
- **Term Loan**: For machinery, equipment, expansion
- **Overdraft Facility**: Withdraw as needed, pay interest on utilized amount
- **MSME Mudra Loan**: Up to ₹10 lakhs without collateral
- **Trade Finance**: For import/export businesses
- **Bill Discounting**: Against invoices and receivables

## Business Sectors Covered
- Manufacturing units
- Trading and wholesale business
- Retail shops and showrooms
- Service providers (IT, consulting, etc.)
- Restaurants and food businesses
- Healthcare clinics and diagnostic centers
- Educational institutions
- Construction contractors
- Transport and logistics

## Benefits
- Quick approval within 5-7 days
- Minimal documentation
- Doorstep service for documentation
- No hidden charges
- Flexible repayment schedule
- Top-up facility on existing loan
- Business advisory services
- Credit guarantee scheme (CGTMSE) for collateral-free loans

## Required Documents
- GST certificate
- Business registration certificate (Udyog Aadhaar, partnership deed, MOA/AOA)
- Last 2 years ITR with computation
- Last 12 months bank statement (current account)
- Last 2 years audited financial statements
- PAN Card and Aadhaar Card of promoters
- Business address proof
- Property documents (if offering collateral)
- Existing loan statements
- Stock statement and receivables list

## Government Schemes
- **MUDRA Loan**: Up to ₹10 lakhs without collateral
- **PMEGP Scheme**: Subsidy for new manufacturing units
- **Stand-Up India**: For SC/ST/Women entrepreneurs
- **CGTMSE**: Credit guarantee for collateral-free loans up to ₹2 crores
- **CLCSS**: Capital subsidy for technology upgradation

## Interest Subsidy
- Women-owned businesses: 0.25% rate reduction
- SC/ST entrepreneurs: Interest subvention available
- North-East region: Special interest rates
- Green/Eco-friendly businesses: 0.5% discount

## Repayment
- EMI based on loan type and tenure
- Moratorium period available for term loans (3-6 months)
- Overdraft: Pay interest monthly, principal on demand
- Seasonal business: Customized EMI schedules
- Step-up/Step-down EMI available
- Prepayment and foreclosure allowed

## Collateral Requirements
- Loans up to ₹10 lakhs: No collateral (under CGTMSE)
- ₹10-50 lakhs: Primary security (stock, receivables, machinery)
- Above ₹50 lakhs: Collateral security (property mortgage)
- Personal guarantee of promoters mandatory
