# Unsecured Loan - Product Brochure

## Overview
DemoBank Unsecured Loan provides quick financing without collateral or guarantor. Perfect for urgent personal needs, business working capital, or debt consolidation.

## Key Features
- Loan Amount: ₹50,000 to ₹25,00,000
- Tenure: 12 to 60 months
- Interest Rate: 12% - 20% p.a. (based on credit profile)
- Processing Fee: 2.5% of loan amount
- No collateral or security required
- No end-use restrictions
- Top-up facility available

## Eligibility Criteria
- Minimum Age: 25 years
- Maximum Age: 58 years
- Minimum Monthly Income: ₹30,000 for salaried
- Minimum Annual Turnover: ₹15 lakhs for self-employed
- Work Experience: Minimum 3 years
- Credit Score: 750+ mandatory
- No existing loan defaults

## Benefits
- Approval within 24 hours
- Disbursal in 48 hours
- No hypothecation or mortgage
- Flexible EMI options
- Part-payment allowed
- Balance transfer facility
- Digital documentation

## Use Cases
- Wedding expenses
- Medical emergencies
- Home renovation
- Debt consolidation
- Business working capital
- Travel and vacation
- Education expenses
- Consumer durable purchase

## Required Documents
- PAN Card
- Aadhaar Card
- Last 6 months salary slips
- Last 12 months bank statement
- Latest Form 16
- Employment verification letter
- Current address proof
- Existing loan statements (if any)

## Repayment
- Fixed EMI throughout tenure
- Auto-debit facility mandatory
- Part-payment allowed after 12 months
- No foreclosure charges after 18 months
- Bullet repayment option for businesses
- EMI holiday available (subject to approval)

## Credit Score Impact
- Timely repayment improves credit score
- Pre-approved limit based on payment history
- Regular top-up offers for good customers
- Lower interest rates on repeat loans
