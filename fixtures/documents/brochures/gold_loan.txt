# Gold Loan - Product Brochure

## Overview
DemoBank Gold Loan provides instant liquidity against your gold jewelry and coins. Get cash within 30 minutes with minimal documentation.

## Key Features
- Loan Amount: ₹10,000 to ₹1,00,00,000
- Loan to Value (LTV): Up to 75% of gold value
- Interest Rate: 7.5% - 10% p.a.
- Tenure: 3 months to 36 months
- Processing Fee: 1% of loan amount (min ₹500)
- No prepayment charges
- Overdraft facility available

## Eligibility Criteria
- Minimum Age: 18 years
- Maximum Age: 70 years
- Gold purity: Minimum 18 karat
- Accepts gold jewelry, coins, and bars
- No income proof required
- Salaried, self-employed, farmers all eligible

## Benefits
- Instant disbursal within 30 minutes
- Free gold purity testing using XRF machine
- Minimal documentation (just ID proof)
- Secure storage in bank vault with insurance
- Part-release facility available
- Interest-only EMI option
- Gold returned in same condition

## Accepted Gold Items
- Yellow gold jewelry (22K, 20K, 18K)
- Gold coins and bars (24K, 22K)
- Gold ornaments (with or without stones)
- Antique gold jewelry
- Note: Stones and other metals excluded from valuation

## Required Documents
- PAN Card or Aadhaar Card
- Passport size photograph
- Address proof (optional)
- Gold ownership proof (optional, but preferred)

## Repayment Options
- Regular EMI (principal + interest)
- Bullet repayment (pay all at maturity)
- Interest servicing (pay interest monthly, principal at end)
- Overdraft facility (pay interest only on utilized amount)

## Gold Safety
- Stored in bank vault with 24/7 CCTV surveillance
- Comprehensive insurance coverage
- Fire and theft protection
- Gold returned in sealed packet after loan closure
