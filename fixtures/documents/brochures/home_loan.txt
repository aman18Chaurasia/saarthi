# Home Loan - Product Brochure

## Overview
Fulfill your dream of owning a home with DemoBank Home Loan. We offer competitive interest rates and flexible repayment options for both under-construction and ready-to-move properties.

## Key Features
- Loan Amount: Up to ₹1 Crore (can go higher based on income)
- Tenure: Up to 20 years (240 months)
- Interest Rate: 8.5% - 11% p.a. (floating)
- LTV Ratio: Up to 90% of property value
- Processing Fee: 0.5% of loan amount

## Eligibility Criteria
- Minimum Age: 21 years
- Maximum Age: 65 years at loan maturity
- Minimum Monthly Income: ₹25,000
- Property Type: Residential (apartment/villa/plot)
- Credit Score: 700+ preferred

## Benefits
- Tax benefits under Section 80C and 24(b)
- Balance transfer facility available
- Top-up loan option for existing customers
- Free property valuation
- Doorstep documentation pickup

## Required Documents
- PAN Card, Aadhaar Card
- Last 6 months salary slips
- Last 12 months bank statements
- Property documents (sale agreement, NOC)
- Approved building plan

## Special Offers
- Women borrowers: 0.05% rate discount
- Salaried professionals: Processing fee waiver
- First-time home buyers: Special rates
