# Commercial Vehicle Loan - Product Brochure

## Overview
DemoBank Commercial Vehicle Loan finances trucks, buses, tippers, taxis, and other commercial vehicles. Expand your transport business or start a new venture.

## Key Features
- Loan Amount: ₹5,00,000 to ₹1,00,00,000
- Margin Money: 10-25% of vehicle on-road price
- Tenure: Up to 7 years
- Interest Rate: 10.5% - 14% p.a.
- Processing Fee: 1.5% of loan amount
- New and used vehicles financed
- Multi-vehicle financing available

## Eligibility Criteria
- Minimum Age: 21 years
- Maximum Age: 65 years
- Minimum Annual Income/Turnover: ₹3 lakhs
- Business vintage: Minimum 2 years for used vehicle
- Valid driving license
- Credit Score: 650+
- Self-employed, partnership, or company

## Vehicles Covered
- Light Commercial Vehicles (LCV): Tata Ace, Mahindra Bolero Pickup
- Medium Commercial Vehicles: Eicher, Ashok Leyland trucks
- Heavy Commercial Vehicles: Tata Prima, Volvo trucks
- Buses: Tourist, school, and public transport buses
- Tippers and dumpers
- Tankers (water, fuel, milk)
- Container trucks
- Used commercial vehicles (up to 5 years old)

## Benefits
- Finance up to 90% of vehicle on-road price
- Hypothecation in favor of bank
- Comprehensive insurance included
- GPS tracking facility available
- Fast-track approval for fleet expansion
- Doorstep documentation
- Special rates for transport associations

## Required Documents
- PAN Card and Aadhaar Card
- Driving License (mandatory)
- Business proof (GST certificate, shop license)
- Last 2 years ITR
- Last 12 months bank statement
- Vehicle quotation from dealer
- Address proof (residence and business)
- Passport size photographs

## Repayment
- Fixed EMI based on vehicle price and tenure
- Auto-debit from bank account
- Step-down EMI available (higher EMI initially)
- Prepayment allowed after 6 months
- Foreclosure charges: 4% of outstanding principal
- Swap old vehicle for new (exchange program)

## Insurance & Registration
- Comprehensive insurance mandatory for loan period
- Bank added as hypothecation holder in RC
- Transfer of ownership requires NOC from bank
- Insurance claim settlement assistance
- Annual insurance renewal reminder

## Vehicle Financing Matrix
- New LCV: 90% funding, 7 years tenure
- New MCV/HCV: 85% funding, 7 years tenure
- Used vehicles (< 3 years): 75% funding, 5 years tenure
- Used vehicles (3-5 years): 60% funding, 3 years tenure
- Multi-vehicle purchase: Customized schemes
