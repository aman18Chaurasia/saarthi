# Education Loan - Product Brochure

## Overview
DemoBank Education Loan helps you achieve your academic dreams in India and abroad. We finance undergraduate, postgraduate, and professional courses at top institutions.

## Key Features
- Loan Amount: ₹50,000 to ₹50,00,000
- Tenure: Up to 15 years
- Interest Rate: 9.5% - 12% p.a. (based on course and institution)
- Processing Fee: 1% of loan amount (max ₹10,000)
- No collateral required for loans up to ₹7.5 lakhs
- Interest payment can be deferred until course completion + 1 year

## Eligibility Criteria
- Minimum Age: 18 years
- Maximum Age: 35 years at loan maturity
- Student must have admission confirmation from recognized institution
- Co-applicant (parent/guardian) required
- Academic record: Minimum 50% in last qualifying exam

## Coverage
- Tuition fees (100%)
- Hostel charges
- Books and equipment
- Travel expenses (for studying abroad)
- Study material and laptops
- Exam fees and thesis costs
- Building funds and caution deposits

## Benefits
- Moratorium period: Course duration + 1 year
- Tax benefits under Section 80E
- Flexible repayment options
- Top-up loans available
- Covers courses in India and abroad

## Required Documents
- Admission letter from institution
- Fee structure from college
- Academic transcripts (10th, 12th, graduation)
- PAN Card and Aadhaar Card (student + co-applicant)
- Income proof of co-applicant
- Last 6 months bank statement
- Property documents (for loans above ₹7.5 lakhs)

## Repayment
- EMI starts after moratorium period ends
- Flexible tenure up to 15 years
- Prepayment allowed without penalty
- Step-up EMI option available (lower EMIs initially)
