# Credit Card - Product Brochure

## Overview
DemoBank Premium Credit Card offers rewards, cashback, and benefits on every purchase. Enjoy shopping, dining, travel, and entertainment perks.

## Key Features
- Credit Limit: ₹50,000 to ₹10,00,000 (based on income)
- Interest Rate: 3.5% per month (42% p.a.) on outstanding balance
- Annual Fee: ₹500 (waived if annual spend > ₹1,50,000)
- Interest-free period: Up to 50 days
- Contactless payment enabled
- EMI conversion available

## Eligibility Criteria
- Minimum Age: 21 years
- Maximum Age: 65 years
- Minimum Monthly Income: ₹20,000
- Credit Score: 700+ preferred
- Salaried or self-employed

## Rewards & Cashback
- 2% cashback on all online purchases
- 5% cashback on groceries and dining
- 10X reward points on weekend spends
- 1 reward point = ₹0.25
- No capping on cashback earnings
- Bonus 5,000 points on first transaction

## Travel Benefits
- 4 complimentary airport lounge access per year (domestic)
- 2 international lounge access per year
- Travel insurance up to ₹10 lakhs
- Lost baggage cover: ₹50,000
- Flight delay compensation
- Hotel and flight booking discounts

## Dining & Entertainment
- Buy 1 Get 1 free at partner restaurants (monthly)
- Movie ticket discounts (BookMyShow, PVR)
- Streaming service offers (Netflix, Amazon Prime)
- Concert and event ticket priority booking

## Additional Benefits
- Zero liability on lost card transactions
- Fuel surcharge waiver at all petrol pumps
- Utility bill payment cashback
- EMI conversion on purchases above ₹5,000
- Add-on cards for family members (free)

## Required Documents
- PAN Card
- Aadhaar Card
- Last 3 months salary slips
- Last 6 months bank statement
- Passport size photograph
- Form 16 (for self-employed)

## Fees & Charges
- Annual Fee: ₹500 (first year free)
- Late payment fee: ₹500
- Over limit fee: ₹600
- Cash advance fee: 2.5% of amount
- Foreign currency markup: 3.5%
- Card replacement: ₹100
