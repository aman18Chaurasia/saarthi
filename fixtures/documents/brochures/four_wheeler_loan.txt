# Four Wheeler Loan (Car Loan) - Product Brochure

## Overview
DemoBank Four Wheeler Loan makes your dream car affordable. Finance new and used cars from all major brands with attractive interest rates and flexible tenure.

## Key Features
- Loan Amount: ₹2,00,000 to ₹1,00,00,000
- Margin Money: 10-20% of car on-road price
- Tenure: Up to 7 years
- Interest Rate: 8.5% - 11% p.a.
- Processing Fee: 1% of loan amount (max ₹5,000)
- 100% on-road price financing for select models
- Special festive offers

## Eligibility Criteria
- Minimum Age: 21 years
- Maximum Age: 65 years
- Minimum Monthly Income: ₹25,000 (salaried), ₹4 lakhs annual turnover (self-employed)
- Work Experience: Minimum 2 years
- Credit Score: 700+
- Valid driving license preferred

## Vehicle Coverage
- New cars: All brands (Maruti, Hyundai, Tata, Honda, Toyota, etc.)
- Used cars: Up to 5 years old
- Hatchbacks, Sedans, SUVs, MUVs
- Electric vehicles (EVs) with special rates
- Hybrid vehicles
- Luxury segment cars (higher down payment)

## Benefits
- Pre-approved loans for existing customers
- Doorstep documentation
- Tie-ups with 500+ dealers across India
- Free vehicle insurance for first year (select models)
- Extended warranty options
- Exchange old car for new (best exchange value)
- Balance transfer from other banks

## Required Documents
- PAN Card
- Aadhaar Card
- Last 3 months salary slips
- Last 6 months bank statement
- Form 16 or ITR (last 2 years)
- Vehicle quotation from dealer
- Driving license (if available)
- Passport size photographs

## Interest Rate Structure
- Salaried customers: 8.5% - 9.5% p.a.
- Self-employed: 9.5% - 10.5% p.a.
- Used cars: +1% over new car rates
- Women borrowers: 0.05% discount
- Electric vehicles: 0.25% discount
- Green vehicles (CNG/Hybrid): 0.10% discount

## Repayment Options
- Fixed EMI throughout loan tenure
- Balloon EMI (pay lump sum at end, lower monthly EMI)
- Step-up EMI (lower EMI initially, increases annually)
- Bullet repayment (for businesses)
- Part-payment allowed anytime without charges
- Foreclosure allowed after 6 months (2% charge)

## Special Schemes
- Festival Bonanza: 0.5% rate discount during Diwali, New Year
- Women's Day: Special rates for women borrowers
- Year-end clearance: Dealer-tied offers
- Corporate tie-ups: Pre-negotiated rates for employees
- Doctor's scheme: Special rates for medical professionals
- Defence personnel: Exclusive benefits

## Insurance & Registration
- Comprehensive insurance mandatory
- Zero depreciation cover available
- Extended warranty options
- Registration charges included in loan
- RTO tax and road tax financed
- Accessories and modifications financed
