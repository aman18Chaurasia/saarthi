# Loan Against Property (LAP) - Product Brochure

## Overview
DemoBank Loan Against Property helps you unlock the value of your residential or commercial property. Use the funds for business expansion, education, medical needs, or debt consolidation.

## Key Features
- Loan Amount: ₹5,00,000 to ₹5,00,00,000
- Loan to Value (LTV): Up to 65% of property market value
- Tenure: Up to 20 years
- Interest Rate: 9.5% - 13% p.a.
- Processing Fee: 1% of loan amount (max ₹25,000)
- Residential and commercial properties accepted
- Top-up facility on existing LAP

## Eligibility Criteria
- Minimum Age: 25 years
- Maximum Age: 70 years at loan maturity
- Minimum Monthly Income: ₹25,000 (salaried), ₹3 lakhs turnover (self-employed)
- Property must be in applicant's name or co-applicant
- Clear title and approved property
- Credit Score: 650+

## Property Types Accepted
- Self-occupied residential property
- Rented residential property
- Commercial office space
- Commercial shops and showrooms
- Industrial property and warehouses
- Residential plots in approved layouts
- Under-construction properties (limited cases)

## Benefits
- Lowest interest rates (secured loan)
- Long repayment tenure reduces EMI burden
- Retain property ownership and usage
- Rental income can continue
- No end-use restrictions on loan amount
- Tax benefits on interest paid (for business use)
- Balance transfer from other banks

## Required Documents
- Property documents (sale deed, title deed, encumbrance certificate)
- Property tax receipts
- Approved building plan
- Latest property valuation report
- PAN Card and Aadhaar Card
- Income proof (salary slips, ITR, bank statements)
- Existing loan statements (if any)
- NOC from society/builder

## Property Valuation
- Free property valuation by bank-approved valuers
- Valuation valid for 6 months
- Market value determines loan eligibility
- Location, age, and condition impact valuation
- Agricultural land not accepted

## Loan Utilization
- Business expansion and working capital
- Debt consolidation
- Children's education (higher studies)
- Medical treatment expenses
- Wedding expenses
- Property purchase or construction
- Any personal or business need

## Repayment
- Fixed or floating interest rate options
- Overdraft facility available
- Step-up EMI for growing businesses
- Balloon payment option
- Prepayment allowed with nominal charges
- Foreclosure allowed after 12 months
